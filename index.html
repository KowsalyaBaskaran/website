About us

Who We Are

The foundation of the K.S.RANGASAMY COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY was laid in the year 1994, near Tiruchengode, South India. A vital part of S enormous K.S.R. Group of Institutions, it is carving the distinct place for itself under the tutelage of Lion Dr. K. S. Rangasamy. To Know, To Serve, To Rise through the process of improvement has been the guiding mission behind the institution. The Zealous Information Technology Association (ZITA) functions with basic objective of providing an excellent platform for students for their Co-Curricular activities, such as conducting inter-college technical symposia, technical quizzes, software contests and Intra departmental competitions on technical aspects to produce eminent professionals tuned to the real time working environment the ever expanding IT industry.
