CONNECTED INDIA CHALLENGE

  • DescriptionOpen or Close

    At the outset of an era where people and machines are more connected than ever before, PROBE presents to the aspiring innovator, an IoT Competition.

    PROBE, the technical symposium of the ECE Department at NIT Trichy, takes pride in launching the Connected India Challenge sponsored by Texas Instruments India.

    We invite all visionaries to send in original ideas on using IoT to innovate
    1. Our homes,
    2. Our cities,
    3. Our environment,
    4. Our entire nation.

    Participants get an opportunity to design and prototype IoT solutions using Development Kits from Texas Instruments. Check below for the event format. Don’t miss your chance to cash in on prizes worth INR 25,000.

  • Prize MoneyOpen or Close

    Prizes worth INR 25,000.

  • FormatOpen or Close

    Abstract Submission Phase:
    Teams will have to submit an abstract with a work plan for their proposed project via email to events.probe17@gmail.com with IOT_<registered team name> as the subject by 30th January 2017. The teams with innovative and feasible proposals will be selected for the working prototype phase.

    Prototype Building Phase:
    Shortlisted teams need to come to NIT Trichy during Probe 2017 between 4th and 5th February 2017. Each team will be given 30 hours to build their prototype in one of the best research labs of NIT Trichy. Further, each team will be provided with Texas Instruments CC3200 Development Kit to build the working prototype. Use of non-TI processors will lead to disqualification. We do not provide other components. The teams need to bring other necessary sensors and components required for their prototype.

    Prototype Demonstration Phase:
    Teams will have to demonstrate their project before a panel of expert judges on 5th February 2017 at 3PM.

  • RulesOpen or Close

    1. Maximum of four individuals permitted per team

    2. Register as a team in the below registration section

    3. Use of non-TI processors will lead to disqualification

    4. Jury's decision will be final

  • Important DatesOpen or Close

    Deadline for abstract submission: 30th January 2017

    Prototype Building: 4th - 5th February 2017

    Final Demonstration: 5th February 2017, 3PM
